New way of traveling outside the Earth discovered.

The researchers say it will reduce travel time in half.

Recent studies at NASAfacilities have revealed the existence of a new chemical compound that can be used as a rocket fuel with a yield exceeding 97%.

Tetracarbonate dioxodestrin, here in after TCDOD, is a compound obtained mainly from the synthesis of various elements of eucalyptus. After 12 years of research, it has finally been able to synthesize it with sufficient purity to achieve a stable fuel.


Day to day applications

At the moment, the fuel requires special deposits to be stored, due to its high storage pressure. However,a possible adaptation to the usual means of transport, starting with the plane,is not ruled out. is not ruled out.

Prof. Marshall, TCDOD chief investigator, says that it is the fuel of the future given its low cost and high performance, but above all because it is a renewable fuel and hardly generates waste.
